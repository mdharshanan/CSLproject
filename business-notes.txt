Business Notes

Marketing:
- Understand customers before planning campaigns.
- Track SEO, social media, email and campaign results.

Finance:
- Study revenue, expenses, profit, loss and cash flow.
- Use budgets and statements to understand business health.

Management:
- Plan tasks, assign responsibilities and monitor progress.
- Good management balances time, people and resources.

Leadership:
- Communicate clearly and listen actively.
- Make decisions, motivate teams and accept responsibility.
